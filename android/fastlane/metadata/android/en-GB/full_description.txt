All closing schedules (for the passage of ships or for maintenance) are available in Chabo! The application also allows you to be notified of upcoming events up to a week in advance!
Several types of notifications are available:
  - At the time of closing
  - At the time of opening
  - The day of closing at the time you want
  - One day before closing at the time you want
  - A summary of upcoming closures for the coming week
